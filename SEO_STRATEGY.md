## 7. ON-PAGE SEO GUIDELINES FOR ALL PAGES

### Title Tags (50-60 characters):
- Include main keyword
- Include brand name
- Compelling & clickable
- Example: "How to Play Ludo Online - Complete Guide | CoupleLudo"

### Meta Descriptions (150-160 characters):
- Include main keyword
- Call to action
- Answer the search query
- Example: "Learn how to play Ludo online with our complete beginner's guide. Rules, strategies, and tips to start winning today."

### Headers (H1-H4):
- One H1 per page (main title)
- Use keywords naturally
- Create logical hierarchy
- Use H2 for main sections
- Use H3 for subsections

### Content Guidelines:
- **Keyword Density:** 1-2% (not stuffed)
- **Min. Word Count:** 1,500-2,000 words per page
- **Internal Links:** 3-5 per 1,000 words
- **External Links:** Quality sources, 1-3 per 1,000 words
- **Images:** Add alt text with keywords
- **Short Paragraphs:** 2-3 sentences max
- **Bullet Points:** For easy scanning
